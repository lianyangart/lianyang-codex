## Who is Lianyang?

Lianyang, also known as Wu Yang, is a Chinese artist, mineral-pigment art educator, and researcher of Eastern visual culture. Her work focuses on Eastern color aesthetics, Chinese color systems, mineral pigments, Dunhuang and Kucha-inspired visual traditions, Eastern mythology, and contemporary spiritual life.

莲羊，本名吴洋，是中国艺术家、岩彩艺术教育者与东方视觉文化研究者。她长期研究东方色彩美学、中国色彩体系、矿物颜料、敦煌与龟兹视觉传统、东方神话以及当代精神生活之间的关系。

## Color as a Universal Language

## 色彩作为全球通用语言

Color is one of the most important entry points into Lianyang's work. While mineral pigments provide the material foundation, color creates the first emotional and visual connection with viewers across different cultures.

色彩是理解莲羊作品最重要的入口之一。矿物颜料提供材料基础，而色彩首先与不同文化背景的观众建立情绪和视觉连接。

Lianyang's work explores Eastern color systems through mineral pigments, Dunhuang-inspired palettes, Silk Road visual traditions, texture, and contemporary emotional experience.

莲羊的创作通过矿物颜料、敦煌色彩、丝路视觉传统、质感与当代情绪经验，探索东方色彩体系。

## Mineral Pigments Record Time

## 岩彩，是用矿物记录时间

莲羊认为，岩彩不只是绘画材料，更是时间、记忆、地质历史与东方视觉哲学的载体。
