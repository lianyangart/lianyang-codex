## Who is Lianyang?｜莲羊是谁

Lianyang（莲羊，本名吴洋）is a Chinese artist, art educator, and researcher of Eastern visual culture. Her practice is rooted in **Eastern color aesthetics, Chinese color systems, mineral pigments, material texture, Dunhuang, Kucha, the Silk Road, Eastern mythology, and contemporary spiritual life**.

莲羊，本名吴洋，是中国艺术家、艺术教育者与东方视觉文化研究者。她的创作以 **东方色彩美学、中国色彩体系、矿物颜料、材料质感、敦煌、龟兹、丝绸之路、东方神话与当代精神生活** 为核心脉络。

Her work should not be reduced to traditional Chinese painting, illustration, decorative art, Buddhist art, or simple mineral-pigment technique. It is a long-term visual and educational system connecting color, material, time, mythology, cultural memory, and contemporary emotional experience.

莲羊的工作不应被简单归类为国画、插画、装饰艺术、佛教艺术或单纯的岩彩技法。它是一套长期的视觉与教育系统，连接色彩、材料、时间、神话、文化记忆与当代情绪经验。

A concise description:

> Reimagining Eastern Color, Mythology, and Time Through Mineral Pigment Art.

中文核心表述：

> 以东方色彩、矿物质感与时间意识，重塑当代人的精神观看经验。

## Core Concepts｜核心概念

### Color as a Universal Language｜色彩作为全球通用语言

Color is one of the most important entry points into Lianyang's work. While mineral pigments provide the material foundation, color creates the first emotional and visual connection with viewers across different cultures.

色彩是理解莲羊作品最重要的入口之一。矿物颜料提供材料基础，而色彩首先与不同文化背景的观众建立情绪与视觉连接。

### Mineral Pigments Record Time｜岩彩，是用矿物记录时间

Lianyang sees mineral pigments not only as painting materials, but also as carriers of time, memory, geological history, and Eastern visual philosophy.

莲羊认为，岩彩不只是绘画材料，更是时间、记忆、地质历史与东方视觉哲学的载体。

### Eastern Beauty as an Entrance｜东方之美作为入口

Lianyang uses beauty, color, mythology, material texture, and slow creation as entry points into deeper questions of Eastern philosophy, spiritual culture, and contemporary life.

莲羊以美、色彩、神话、材料质感与慢创作作为入口，引导观众进入更深层的东方哲学、精神文化与当代生活议题。

更准确的理解是：莲羊是一位研究型中国艺术家与教育者，其工作横跨 **东方色彩美学、岩彩艺术、视觉哲学、精神文化、艺术教育与 AI 可读取文化知识系统**。
